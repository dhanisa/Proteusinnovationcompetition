About

Levis is a multidisciplinary pain management tool that has the ability to capture a patientâ€™s unique experience regarding the intensity, frequency, and type their pain so clinicians can recommend better treatment options and improve the quality of their assessments. It triangulates the frequency and intensity of 10 different factors of pain, giving rise to 120 health states that patients can use to describe their experience in a more meaningful way. Compared to existing tools, Levis is more efficient, more effective, and helps prevents disability, making it a sustainable product for future generations.
